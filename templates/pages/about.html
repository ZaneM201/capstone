About us!

Welcome to the about page of our F-1 Pick'em application. Here, we are passionate about Formula 1 racing and aim to provide an engaging platform for fans to participate in pick'em contests. Our mission is to enhance the F-1 experience by allowing users to test their knowledge and predictions against fellow enthusiasts. Join us on this thrilling journey through the world of Formula 1!

Rules

  1. All users must have their picks submitted before the start of the first qualifying session (Q1).
  2. Picks cannot be changed after Q1 starts.
  3. Points are awarded based on the accuracy of the picks.
  4. The user with the highest points at the end of the season wins the championship.
  5. In case of a tie, the user with the most correct pole positions wins.
